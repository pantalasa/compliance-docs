# DR Tabletop: Regional Outage

## Scenario

Tabletop discussion walking through a simulated full region outage affecting all services in `us-east-1`. The group discussed detection, communication, failover decision-making, and customer-facing communications.

## Recovery Steps Tested

1. Outage detection and paging procedures.
2. Decision tree for declaring a regional incident.
3. Customer communications via status page and social channels.
4. Failover to `us-west-2` standby environment.
5. Post-recovery validation checklist.
